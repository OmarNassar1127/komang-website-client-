Catalogue

Ready for an unforgettable experience? Contact us via the form to schedule your activities or plan an exciting tour. Let us help you make the most of your day in Bali!

Penataran Agung Lempuyang Temple

Penataran Agung Lempuyang Temple, located on the slopes of Mount Lempuyang in Karangasem, Bali, is a must-visit for its serene atmosphere and cultural significance. Join us to explore this popular destination and experience the spiritual essence of Bali.

Tirta Gangga

Tirta Gangga, a former royal palace located near Mount Agung, is renowned for its water palace. Discover the beauty and history of this majestic site with our guided tours, and immerse yourself in the elegance of the Karangasem Kingdom.

Savana Tianyar

Savana Tianyar offers vast grasslands with views of Mount Agung. Whether it's the yellowish grass of the dry season or the lush green of the rainy season, this destination in Karangasem is perfect for nature lovers. Book now for a unique adventure.

Amed Beach

Amed Beach, located in Karangasem, is famous for its stunning underwater scenery. Ideal for snorkeling and diving enthusiasts, join us to explore the vibrant marine life and crystal-clear waters. A perfect spot for a day of sun and sea.

Virgin Beach

Virgin Beach in Karangasem is known for its pure charm and natural beauty. Enjoy a tranquil escape and bask in the serene environment. Book your visit to this hidden gem and experience the untouched splendor of Bali.

Tenganan Pegringsingan

Tenganan Pegringsingan is a traditional village in Karangasem, Bali. Known for preserving ancient customs, it offers a glimpse into Bali's rich cultural heritage. Join our tours to experience the authentic Bali Aga lifestyle.

Swing Activity

Enjoy the Instagrammable Swing Activity with stunning views of the Tegalalang Rice Terrace. Perfect for thrill-seekers and photo enthusiasts. Book now for an unforgettable experience.

ATV Activity

Experience the excitement of riding an ATV through Bali's scenic landscapes. Suitable for all skill levels, this adventure promises fun and thrills. Reserve your spot today and enjoy the ride.

Rafting Activity

Join our Rafting Activity for a thrilling adventure on Bali's rivers. With stunning natural views, this activity is perfect for adrenaline junkies and nature lovers alike. Book now for a wet and wild experience.

Watersport Activity

Dive into excitement with our Watersport Activity in Bali. Offering a variety of games to pump your adrenaline, this is the ultimate sea adventure. Book now and make a splash with us.

Penglipuran Village

Penglipuran Village, one of the world's cleanest villages, preserves traditional Balinese culture. Explore this picturesque village in Bangli Regency and experience the charm of authentic Balinese life. Join our tour today.

Mount Batur

Mount Batur, an active volcano in Kintamani, offers breathtaking views and an unforgettable climbing experience. Perfect for adventure seekers, join our guided tours to explore this natural wonder.

JEEP Tour

Experience the thrill of our JEEP Tour, exploring the vast sands and black lava flows of Mount Batur. This off-road adventure is perfect for adrenaline junkies. Book now for an unforgettable journey.

Ulun Danu Beratan

Ulun Danu Beratan, a significant water temple in Tabanan, offers stunning lakeside views and serene surroundings. Discover this cultural gem with our guided tours and enjoy the tranquility of Bali.

Lovina Beach

Lovina Beach, located in North Bali, is known for its natural black sand and dolphin-watching tours. Join us to experience the unique charm of this serene beach and its surrounding attractions.

Tanah Lot Temple

Tanah Lot Temple, located in Tabanan, is renowned for its stunning sunset views and cultural significance. Visit this iconic temple with us and capture the beauty of Bali's coastline.

Kecak Dance

Experience the iconic Kecak Dance, performed on the cliffs of Uluwatu. This legendary dance, telling the story of Ramayana, is a must-see for anyone visiting Bali. Book your tickets now for an unforgettable cultural experience.

GWK Cultural Park

Visit the Garuda Wisnu Kencana statue, one of Indonesia's largest cultural landmarks. Located in Bali, this impressive statue stands at 121 meters tall. Join our tours to explore this monumental site and its cultural significance.

Tegenungan Waterfall

Tegenungan Waterfall, located in Kemenuh Village, offers a refreshing escape with its 15-meter drop and lush surroundings. Perfect for nature lovers, book your visit to one of Bali's most famous waterfalls.

Nusa Penida

Nusa Penida, known as the "Blue Paradise Island," offers stunning underwater beauty and exotic landscapes. Join our tours to explore this breathtaking island and its crystal-clear waters.
